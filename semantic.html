Breakdown of an OIL CHANGE

DRAINING THE OLD OIL

Underneath the vehicle, locate the oil drain plug on the oil pan. Lefty loosey will remove the plug. Have a catch can underneath, as the old oil will flow out. Once done, replace the drain plug.

REMOVE THE OLD OIL FILTER

Locate the oil filter (underneath or on top of engine depending on make/model). Use your oil filter wrench to remove the filer. CAUTION, oil will spill from filter. Replace with a new filter...remember RIGHTY TIGHTY

FILL WITH NEW OIL

On top of the engine, locate the OIL FILL PORT and add in your oil of choice based on your manufacturers recommendations.

FINISH UP

Close the oil fill port, check your dipstick to ensure proper oil level, run your vehicle and inspect for leaks.

Choosing the proper engine oil may seem complicated. To learn how to decide what oil to use check out Engine Oil 101
